# The Long Way

**The Long Way** is an unconventional navigation app designed for explorers, wanderers, and those who believe that the journey is just as important as the destination.

## The Concept

While traditional navigation apps compete to find the shortest distance or the fastest time, **The Long Way** does the opposite. It is built for those times when you aren't in a rush and want to discover new places, scenic routes, or simply enjoy a longer drive or walk.

Imagine you have 30 minutes to spare on a 10-minute commute. Instead of arriving early, you tell the app: *"I want to arrive in 30 minutes,"* and it will generate a route that fills that time with exploration.

## Key Features

- **Explorative Routing**: Set your destination and define how much extra time or distance you want to spend.
- **Dynamic Theming**: The map style automatically adjusts based on ambient light conditions.
- **Integrated Search**: Powered by Stadia Maps.
- **Current Location Support**: Quick routing from your current position.

## Tech Stack

- **Language**: Kotlin
- **UI Framework**: Jetpack Compose
- **Maps**: MapLibre SDK for Android
- **Data & Geocoding**: Stadia Maps (Valhalla & Pelias)
